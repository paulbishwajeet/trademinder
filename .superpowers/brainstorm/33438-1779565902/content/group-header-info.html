What should the group header show?

Besides the category name, choose what summary info appears on the right side of each group header.

A
▼ WHEEL 6 trades

Trade count only

Simple. Just shows how many trades are in the group (or how many match the current filter).

B
▼ WHEEL 4 open · 2 closed

Status breakdown

Shows count per status (open, closed, expired, assigned) — only non-zero counts shown. Helps you see at a glance how active each category is.

C
▼ WHEEL 4 open · 2 closed  |  P&L: +$312

Status breakdown + total P&L

Status counts plus the sum of unrealized P&L across all trades in the group. More data density at a glance.
