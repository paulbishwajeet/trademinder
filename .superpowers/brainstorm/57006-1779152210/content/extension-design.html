Category dropdown — fetched live from /api/categories

Both the "Add Trade" and "Edit Trade" modals in the extension call /api/categories once when the modal opens. The list is used to populate the dropdown. No hardcoded values in the extension — if you add a new category in the future, it appears automatically.
